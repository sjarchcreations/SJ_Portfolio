About

Experienced Architect with a demonstrated history of working in residential and commercial projects. Received my Bachelor of Architecture in 2020 and currently pursuing Master's degree in "Building Engineering and Management" at School of Planning and Architecture Delhi

Mr. Agarwal’s Residence ,Mohanlal Ganj Lucknow

Residence planned with the idea of proper ventilation to every space in the house.Elevation designed with contemporary theme with the palette of greys and browns. Huge window on the front side at staircase for entering maximum sunlight and plays vital role in natural ventilation.Elements Incorporated the groove in texture , wooden texture HPL sheet ,profile light .

Mr. Siddiqui’s Residence, Gomti Nagar Lucknow

Residence planned on 1500sqft area consist of three floors. Including office at ground floor , residence at first and second floor. Designed on modern theme with the idea of exposed stone material gives elite look.

Mr. Kamal Kandoi’s Residence, Bhilwara Rajasthan

The purpose of this project was to design the residence. It included twelve bedrooms with dresser and toilet , family seating room ,workout room, servant’s room, kitchen, balcony . Revision in planning as per client review regardingVaastu-Shastra and other requirements. Designed different options of 3D model as per client requirement and made changes accordingly in the respective drawings.This work was done at the final year Internship at Viral Munshi Architects, Ahmedabad.

Mr. Khan’s Residence, Biswa

The interior of the bedroom was designed with the intricate detailing work At the bed back wall panel with the continuous pattern of grooving in the veneer. The color palette is brown and grey

Mr. Hasani’s Residence , Lucknow

Drawing room planned as the highlighting aspect of the apartment. The exposed brick wall with the 3d tile pattern to enhance the look. Groove design pattern on the wall with the elongated circular element to design a unique look. Bedroom interior designed with the theme of reflection and use of mirror as bed back wall panel to create an illusion of huge space and simple T.V unit with the linear light in false ceiling.
